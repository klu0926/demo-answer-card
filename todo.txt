- Mini-Map" / Question Navigator:
Problem: If a user skips question 45 to come back to it later, it's hard to find it again by scrolling.
Solution: Add a floating action button (FAB) or a side drawer that shows a grid of tiny squares representing all 200 questions. Color-code them: Grey = Unanswered, Blue = Answered, Red = Flagged. Clicking a square smoothly scrolls them right to that question.

Satisfying Micro-Interactions:
Solution: Add subtle animations. When an option bubble is tapped, it should have a Material Design "ripple" effect or a slight scale "pop" animation. When transitioning between the Setup Steps, slide the content left/right rather than instantly replacing it.


Dark Mode:
Solution: Studying often happens late at night. Implementing a system-aware or toggleable Dark Mode will reduce eye strain and make the app feel incredibly premium.


Auto-Save & Resume:
Idea: Imagine taking a 2-hour test and accidentally refreshing the page. Instead of losing everything, use localStorage to save their current session's answers every time they click a bubble. Allow them to "Resume" an incomplete test.

Data Export/Import (Backup):
Idea: Because localStorage is tied to the browser, clearing cache deletes all test history. Add a simple "Export Data" button that downloads a .json file of their store.js data, and an "Import Data" to restore it on a different device.
